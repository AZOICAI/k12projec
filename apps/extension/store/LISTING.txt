Short description (132 chars max):

Detailed description:
K12 Planner helps online high school students track courses, assignments, study time, and grades in one place at https://k12projec.vercel.app

This extension connects to your account so you can:
• See overdue, due today, and due-soon assignments in the toolbar popup
• Quick-add assignments with course and due date
• Save Google Classroom assignments with one click (title and due date when available)
• Jump to Today, Courses, or the Focus study timer on the website
• Optional badge and notifications for work that needs attention

Sign up on the website, then open extension Options and sign in with the same email and password.
