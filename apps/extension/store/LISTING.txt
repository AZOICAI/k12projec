Name: K12 Planner

Short description (132 chars max):
Add assignments to your K12 Planner account from any tab. See due-soon count on the extension badge.

Detailed description:
K12 Planner helps online high school students track courses, assignments, and study blocks in one place.

This extension connects to your K12 Planner account at https://k12projec.vercel.app so you can:
• Quickly add assignments from the toolbar popup
• Save selected text or Google Classroom assignments to your planner
• See how many assignments are due soon on the badge (optional notifications)
• Sign in once in extension Options and stay synced with the web app

You need a free K12 Planner account. Sign up on the website, then open extension Options to connect.

Permission justifications (if asked):
• storage — Saves your sign-in session and app URL settings locally
• alarms — Refreshes the due-soon assignment badge periodically
• host_permissions (k12projec.vercel.app) — Calls the K12 Planner API to create assignments
• host_permissions (supabase.co) — Signs you in with the same Supabase account as the web app

Single purpose: Quick assignment entry for K12 Planner users.

Data usage: Account credentials and planner data are sent only to your configured K12 Planner backend (Supabase auth + app API). No browsing history is collected.
